How to Play Cryptle

Single Player (1P)

Guess the five-letter word in six tries, just like Wordle:

🟨 Yellow means the letter is correct, but in the wrong spot.

🟩 Green means the letter is correct and in the right spot.

Multi Player (MP)

Guess the words suggested by other players:

🟦 Blue means the letter is in the right spot (for at least one word).

🟪 Purple means you have guessed a word from another player.

🟥 Red means this word was already guessed before (not valid).

Hack Challenge

Guess the words by hacking the server as root:

🔒 Cryptle encrypts data in use: not even the OS has access to it.

👩‍💻 Submit an open source application to run with root privileges.
